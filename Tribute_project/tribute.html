Mahtma Ghandi

The man who freed India

Here is a time line of Mahatma Ghandi's Life:

  • 1869:Born at Porbandar, Kathiawad, son of Karamchand (Kaba) and Putlibai Gandhi.
  • 1876: Attended primary school in Rajkot, where his family moved.
  • 1891: Sailed from Bombay for England to study law.
  • 1893: Sailed for South Africa to become lawyer for an Indian firm.
  • 1893: Found himself subjected to all kinds of color discrimination.
  • 1894: Organised Natal Indian Congress.
  • 1947Began tour of Bihar to lessen Hindu-Moslem tensions.
  • 1948:Fasted for five days in Delhi for communal unity.
  • 1948: Assassinated in 79th year at Birla House by Nathuram Vinayak Godse.
